MISSION

Provide a steady stream of professionally competent and value oriented diplomats
in engineering and secretarial field.
Strengthen existing process and institutional capability through continuing education
programs and training systems.
Assist in quality improvement efforts of polytechnic education.
Develop quality consciousness in all activities of the polytechnic through strategic planning.
Design and implement need based formal, informal and continuing education programs for
the industries and community.
Have close interaction between the institution and industry.
Design and impart training programs for updating knowledge of staff.
Implement the programs for community.
Introduce Management Information System (MIS).

VISION

To become one of the best institution imparting values based, need based,
responsive technical education, to meet the standards of future advancement.
To attain self sustainability & overall development.
To contribute positive and productive thoughts for the overall development of the
institute and also the community, in turn socioeconomic development of our nation.
To serve the poorest of the poor and also to uplift the rural folk through community
development polytechnic activities.
