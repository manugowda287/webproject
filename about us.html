ABOUT US

Government Polytechnic, Channasandra is an AICTE approved institution run by Government
of Karnataka and coming under the purview of Directorate of Technical Education. The
institute is rendering yeomen service to the citizens of Channasandra and surrounding areas
by offering three year diploma program in various engineering and non engineering courses.
The training is rendered by well qualified dedicated staff with supportive infrastructure.


MANDATE



  • Scientific, technical, ethical and value based approaches for effective management and implementation
    of academic activities.

  • Establishment of two- way communication system, interaction and optimum utilization of existing resources.
  • Design and implementation of informal courses under CCTEK for the benefit of community as a whole.

  • Updating the standards of the staff to meet the requirement of stake holders through staff development training programs.

  • Community development through polytechnic activities.

  • Effective management and utilization of the finance of the institute.

  • Provide student services like counseling, placement, interaction with industries, cultural activities, and energy conservation awareness.

  • Creating awareness about effective use of natural resource.
