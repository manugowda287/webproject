Government Polytechnic, Channasandra is an AICTE approved institution run by Government
of Karnataka and coming under the purview of Directorate of Technical Education. The
institute is rendering yeomen service to the citizens of Channasandra and surrounding areas
by offering three year diploma program in various engineering and non engineering courses.
The training is rendered by well qualified dedicated staff with supportive infrastructure.
